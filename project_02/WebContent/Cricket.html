Cricket is a sport that combines strategy, skill, and teamwork, captivating millions of fans worldwide. Played between two teams of eleven players, the game revolves around batting, bowling, and fielding, with the primary goal of scoring more runs than the opposing team. The bowler delivers the ball to the batsman, who attempts to hit it and run between wickets to score, while the fielding team tries to prevent runs and dismiss the batsmen
